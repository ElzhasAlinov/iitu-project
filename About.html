About us

This web site exists since March 21, 1994. Our goal is to deliver comprehensive, up-to-date information on traveling in Japan, first-hand from Japan.

Meet the team

Kojima Hideo

Hideo Kojima, a Tokyo native, found inspiration in Japanese cinema, notably Akira Kurosawa's work. His 1996 game, "Metal Gear Solid," acclaimed for its storytelling and gameplay, catapulted him to fame.
Kojima's collaboration with Japan-Travel.com stemmed from a desire to showcase Japan's cultural richness to the world. As a creative advisor for the platform, he contributed his knowledge of gaming
narratives to craft interactive guides that portrayed travel as an enthralling story. His aim was to attract travelers not just with destinations, but with journeys that felt like immersive games.

Hayao Miyazaki

Hayao Miyazaki, revered for his legendary contributions to Japanese animation, extended his talents beyond filmmaking to collaborate with Japan-Travel.com. Since March 21, 1994, Miyazaki has been an artistic
advisor, enriching the platform with his intricate drawings of Japanese art. His stunning illustrations capture the essence of Japan's landscapes, culture, and traditions, offering travelers a visual journey
through his masterful artwork. Miyazaki's partnership with Japan-Travel.com has been instrumental in enhancing the platform's depiction of Japan's beauty and allure,
showcasing his unparalleled artistic vision to a global audience seeking to explore the country's wonders.

Elzhas Alinov

Elzhas Alinov grew up in Taldyqorgan, Kazakhstan and met his interest in Japan when studying in Vancouver, Canada.
There, he founded japan-travel.com in 1994 before moving the website's operation to Japan in 2003.
